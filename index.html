About Our Initiative

Our initiative, "Free Education for Kids", is dedicated to empowering underprivileged children through free learning opportunities. We provide tutoring in basic subjects, along with guidance in critical thinking, storytelling, arts, and early coding skills.

I personally lead and coordinate these activities as a volunteer, aiming to make education accessible for children who cannot afford it. So far, over 50 children in our community have benefited, developing both academic skills and personal confidence.

Our long-term vision is to expand this initiative, reaching even more children, and ensuring that poverty does not limit their future. Education is a right, not a privilege, and we are committed to making it a reality for those in need.
